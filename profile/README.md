# Subsurface4D

**Imaging and monitoring the Earth's subsurface — from 3D structure to 4D dynamics.**

</div>

Subsurface4D develops open-source frameworks at the intersection of wave physics, fiber-optic sensing, and Bayesian inference. Our goal is to turn subsurface *imaging* into subsurface *monitoring*: continuous, uncertainty-aware pictures of how the Earth changes beneath our feet — with applications spanning carbon storage, volcanic unrest, induced seismicity, and groundwater.
